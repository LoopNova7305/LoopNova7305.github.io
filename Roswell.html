The Roswell Incident

The events

The Roswell incident is one of the most famous alien coverups/incidents ever recorded. In 1947, a msyterious object crashed near Roswell, New Mexico. A rancher by the named of Mac Brazel found on his branch what looked like metallic sticks, glossy, metallic like paper, and weird plastics. Upon calling the sheriffs, soldiers from the nearby Air Force Base came with armored trucks and military vehicles to pick up all the materials. New reports later came out saying the government had captured a flying saucer with alien bodies. This quickly gained traction and may have started the ufology field.
