The Roswell Incident

The events

The Roswell incident is one of the most famous alien coverups/incidents ever recorded. In 1947, a msyterious object crashed near Roswell, New Mexico. A rancher by the named of Mac Brazel found on his branch what looked like metallic sticks, glossy, metallic like paper, and weird plastics. Upon calling the sheriffs, soldiers from the nearby Air Force Base came with armored trucks and military vehicles to pick up all the materials. New reports later came out saying the government had captured a flying saucer with alien bodies. This quickly gained traction and may have started the ufology field.

Due to growing interest over the next few decades, as well as many UFO sightings, millions of people became interested in the Roswell incident. Many people came out and claimed to have worked for the government, and claimed the government was hiding alien technology specimens, and more.

The Witnesses

Upon the initial incident, the Mac Brazel himself came out and said there were alien bodies at the crash site, along with alien metal. Though the alien bodies' existence has yet to be proven, it is true that the metals found at the crash site were strange in origin. They would not bend even after being hit with a sledgehammer. A ufologist by the name of Glenn Dennis later stated that alien autopsies were carried out at Area 51. More recently, in 2008, a flight engineer by the name of William Ennis, who was stationed at Area 51, stated that there was in fact, an alien spacecraft that flew without engines, and that before he died, he would like to find out the whole truth. Though none of these statements truly hold much value, there is more evidence that inevitably points to the truth.

The Compelling Evidence.

There is much evidence that the Roswell incident was nothing more than a weather balloon crash or a balloon crash from Project Mogul, a top-secret U.S experiment to detect sound waves from Soviet nuke tests. However, some evidence suggests otherwise.

A few decades after the Roswell incident, the government released to the public much of the details surrounding Project Mogul, thus proving to many the Roswell incident was nothing more than a hoax. However, when requesting for files about the Roswell incident, it revealed the fact all the files from January 1947 to October 1947 at Roswell had either been destroyed or the government was hiding those files.
